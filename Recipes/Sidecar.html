Sidecar

Helps you forget those 2am texts.

Prep time: 5 minutes

Serving size:1

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 fluid ounces cognac
  • 1 fluid ounce orange-flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau®)
  • ½ fluid ounce lemon juice
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Directions

Step1

Sprinkle sugar onto a small plate. Lightly wet half the rim of a chilled martini glass with a damp paper towel. Dip the moistened rim in sugar to coat..

Step 2

Combine cognac, orange-flavored liqueur, and lemon juice in a cocktail shaker. Add ice, cover, and shake until chilled. Strain into the martini glass.
